About Switzerland

Switzerland is a mountainous Central European country, with numerous lakes, villages and the high peaks of the Alps. The country is also known for its ski resorts and hiking trails.

Picture here

Basic Info

Capital: Bern

Population (as of 2020): 8.637 Million

Currency: Swiss franc

Major Cities: Zürich, Geneva, Basel, Lausanne, Bern, Winterthur, Lucerne, St. Gallen

Official Language(s): German, French, Romansh, Italian

Things You Should Do in Switzerland:

  • Visit Chillon Castle
  • See Rhine Falls, Europe's largest plains waterfall
  • Visit Mount Pilatus, which has amazing views, cable cars and a railway
  • Take a train ride to Jungfraujoch, a high-altitude glacier
  • Visit Lake Zurich, a celebrated lake for boating and swimming
  • Visit the iconic Chapel Bridge, lined with art
  • Visit Jungfrau, an alpine summit with railway and an ice palace
  • And see the Kunsthaus Zürich, a gallery with major European artworks
