About Greece

Greece is a country located in southeastern Europe with thousands of islands throughout the Aegean and Ionian seas. Its capital Athens retains landmarks including the 5th-century B.C. Acropolis citadel with the Parthenon temple. Greece is also known for its beaches, from the black sands of Santorini to the party resorts of Mykonos.

Basic Info

Capital: Athens

Population (as of 2020): 10.72 Million

Currency: Euro

Major Cities: Athens, Thessaloniki, Patras, Larissa, Heraklion, Volos, Ioannina, Serres

Official Language(s): Greek



Things You Should Do in Greece:

  • Visit Acropolis of Athens, a 5th-century B.C. hilltop temple monument
  • Visit Meteora, an UNESCO-listed monastery complex
  • See and take pictures of the Parthenon, the iconic 5th-century B.C. Athenian temple ruins
  • Go to the Acropolis Museum, which houses ancient treasures in a contemporary venue
  • Visit Navagio, the famed turquoise cove with a shipwreck
  • Tour the National Archaeological Museum, home to Ancient Greek art and archaeological finds
  • See the Temple of Olympian Zeus, a ruined temple completed under Hadrian
  • See Delos, Ancient Greek ruins and a sacred site
  • And visit the Ancient Agora of Athens, which are iconic Greek ruins accompanied by a museum
