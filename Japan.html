About Japan

Japan is an island country in East Asia. It is situated in the northwest Pacific Ocean, and is bordered on the west by the Sea of Japan, while extending from the Sea of Okhotsk in the north toward the East China Sea and Taiwan in the south. Japan is a part of the Ring of Fire, and spans an archipelago of 6,852 islands covering 377,975 square kilometers (145,937 sq mi). The five main islands are Hokkaido, Honshu (considered the "mainland"), Shikoku, Kyushu, and Okinawa.

Things You Should Do in Japan

  • Visit and see Mount Fuji
  • Relax and unwind in an Onsen, a hot spring
  • Hike the Kumano Kodo Trail
  • Go Izakaya Hopping
  • Visit the Tokyo National Museum, a grand site for Japanese art and antiques
  • Go to the Osaka Castle, a renowned and historic castle with a museum
  • Tour the Kyoto National Museum, which displays Japanese art and culture since 1897
  • Go to the Himeji Castle, a landmark 17th-century white castle
  • See the Matsumoto Castle, a prominent historical site with exhibits
  • See the Takeda Castle Ruins, which are castle ruins on a scenic mountaintop
  • And visit the Imperial Palace, which is an emperor's residence with gardens and tours
